Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of all your personal data from Posty at any time.

How to delete your data

  1. Open the Posty app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, email us at davidtza100@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Data" and we will process your request.

What gets deleted

All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, including your account information, video recordings, transcripts, generated content, and any connected social media account data.
